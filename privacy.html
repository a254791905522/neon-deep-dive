Neon Deep Dive Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2026

Neon Deep Dive is an offline iPhone game. No personal information is collected, stored, transmitted, sold, or shared.

  • Local progress may be stored only on device so completed dives and unlocked levels can be restored.
  • No advertising SDK, analytics SDK, tracking SDK, social login, or in-app purchase SDK is included.
  • The game does not require an account and does not send gameplay data to a server.
